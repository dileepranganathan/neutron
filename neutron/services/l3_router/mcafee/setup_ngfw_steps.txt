[1mDESCRIPTION[0m[m
       The QEMU PC System emulator simulates the following peripherals:[m
[m
       -   i440FX host PCI bridge and PIIX3 PCI to ISA bridge[m
[m
       -   Cirrus CLGD 5446 PCI VGA card or dummy VGA card with Bochs VESA extensions (hardware level, including all non standard modes).[m
[m
       -   PS/2 mouse and keyboard[m
[m
       -   2 PCI IDE interfaces with hard disk and CD-ROM support[m
[m
       -   Floppy disk[m
[m
       -   PCI and ISA network adapters[m
[m
       -   Serial ports[m
[m
       -   Creative SoundBlaster 16 sound card[m
[m
       -   ENSONIQ AudioPCI ES1370 sound card[m
[m
       -   Intel 82801AA AC97 Audio compatible sound card[m
[m
       -   Intel HD Audio Controller and HDA codec[m
[m
       -   Adlib (OPL2) - Yamaha YM3812 compatible chip[m
[m
       -   Gravis Ultrasound GF1 sound card[m
[m
       -   CS4231A compatible sound card[m
[m
[7m Manual page qemu(1) line 1 (press h for help or q to quit)[27m[K[K       -   PCI UHCI USB controller and a virtual USB hub.[m
[m
       SMP is supported with up to 255 CPUs.[m
[m
       QEMU uses the PC BIOS from the Seabios project and the Plex86/Bochs LGPL VGA BIOS.[m
[m
       QEMU uses YM3812 emulation by Tatsuyuki Satoh.[m
[m
       QEMU uses GUS emulation (GUSEMU32 <[1mhttp://www.deinmeister.de/gusemu/[0m>) by Tibor "TS" SchÃ¼tz.[m
[m
       Note that, by default, GUS shares [4mIRQ[24m(7) with parallel ports and so QEMU must be told to not have parallel ports to have working GUS.[m
[m
               qemu-system-i386 dos.img -soundhw gus -parallel none[m
[m
       Alternatively:[m
[m
               qemu-system-i386 dos.img -device gus,irq=5[m
[m
       Or some other unclaimed IRQ.[m
[m
       CS4231A is the chip used in Windows Sound System and GUSMAX products[m
